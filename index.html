Synthesizing Vowel-Like Tones with Pitch Circularity

Kaori Hashimoto, Takao Kawamura, Nobutaka Ono

Sound Demos

This section presents musical examples composed of the pitch-circular vowel tones [1]. The normal vowels were synthesized using Sinsy [2], and then pitch-manipulated using WORLD [3]. For more details, please refer to our paper.

Boléro / Maurice Ravel (Composed)

Both normal vowel and pitch-circular vowel tone use 12 semitone-spaced tones within one octave (C3 to B3). Note that the original score of ``Boléro'' spans 14 semitones (C3 to D4), although the interval between adjacent tones is never more than five semitones.
